Intention:

  • This data art visualizes information about asteroids fetched from the NASA API ranging between specific dates (August 25th, 2023 to September 1st, 2023).
  • The intention is to screate an interactive and visually appealing representation of Asteroid Data, using D3.js.
  • The Data Driven Artwork conveys information about asteroids near earth and shows their speeds in animation and their sizes over a given period of time.
  • It shows trends and asteroids attributes over time, allowing for users to explore its speeds and sizes, as users can analyze how each asteroid speed and size change and differ over time.
  • The animated transitions demonstrates the movements of the asteroids in space, however within the visualization.

Style Guide

  • Color: Each of the asteroids are blue, however when you hover over them they turn Orange.
